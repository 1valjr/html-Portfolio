The Best Series According to Humval

My top 3 series of all-time.

1. Dark (2017-2020)

I love a science fiction thriller. Dark is a story follows characters from the fictional town of Winden, Germany, as they pursue the truth in the aftermath of a child's disappearance.

2. Game of Thrones

I loved this series and I intend to watch it again when I have more free time =).

3. Travelers

Travelers is a science fiction television series. In a post-apocalyptic future, thousands of special operatives are tasked with preventing the collapse of society. These operatives, known as "travelers", have their consciousnesses sent back in time and transferred into the "host" body of present-day individuals who are about to die, minimizing unexpected impact on the future.
